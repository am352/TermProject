About Us:

CampusBooks is a mock online shop that helps students find affordable used textbooks.

Our mission is to keep textbooks in circulation and save students money every semester. No more sleepless nights looking for a working pdf!

CampusBooks was inspired by real life stories of inordinent textbook costs, the inability to find readily available, affordable options, and the need for a network to exist for students to share their previous books online; who needs eBay, anyways?

We do our best to offer affordable prices to students like you (and like us!)
